UseYourTools for Work
Version 1.0 — MVP

Your workday, in one place. UseYourTools for Work brings together your calendar, daily planner, and caseload into a fast, browser-native tool — no installs, no accounts, no friction. Inspired by the UseYourTools iOS app.

What's working
📅 Daily Planner with auto-saved time blocks
🏠 Dashboard with live clock & daily quote
📆 Google Calendar — live events on dashboard
📬 Gmail — unread count with inbox link
🎨 5 color themes + dark mode
💾 All data saved locally — no account needed
Coming soon
🗂️ Cases — JIRA & Salesforce caseload
📄 PDF schedule export
